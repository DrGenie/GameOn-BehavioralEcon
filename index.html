Ultimatum Game Simulation

Objective: Investigate fairness norms and rejection thresholds.

Pair up as proposers and responders to split \$10. Rejections often occur if offers are too low (Camerer, 2003).

Trust Game Roleplay

Objective: Observe reciprocity. Amounts sent get tripled, then partially returned (Camerer, 2003).

Framing Effects in Public Goods

Objective: Compare cooperation between “Community Fund” vs. “Stock Market” frames (Angner, 2016).

Speculative Bubbles Market Simulation

Objective: Recreate rapid price inflation and crash, reflecting a bubble cycle (Kahneman & Tversky, 1979).

Rock-Paper-Scissors Strategy Showdown

Objective: Practice level-K reasoning by anticipating others’ moves (Camerer, 2003).
